About Webstorm

The Webstorm Hackathon revolves around the art of problem-solving. Participants are encouraged to address a specific challenge within predefined categories, showcasing their ingenuity and creativity. The objective is to devise a distinctive, innovative solution that stands out among the rest.

In this hackathon, participants have the freedom to choose a problem they believe is impactful within the provided categories. Whether it's related to web development, user experience, or any other relevant field, the emphasis is on creating something unique and groundbreaking.
